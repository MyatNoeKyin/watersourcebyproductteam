What is WaterSource functional water?

WaterSource focuses on hydrogen-rich alkaline functional water. Its systems are positioned around eight water properties: dissolved hydrogen, moderate alkaline pH, negative ORP, smaller water clusters, oxygenated alkaline ionized water, ionic minerals, filtration and external-use acidic water settings.

How is WaterSource different from a normal water dispenser?

A normal dispenser mainly provides hot or cold drinking water. WaterSource is presented as a functional water system that combines filtration with hydrogen-rich alkaline water, mineral balance and specialist-guided product matching.

Which WaterSource system should I choose?

The right model depends on your home or office space, usage habits, installation preference, hot-water needs and wellness goals. A WaterSource specialist can help compare the five options.

What do hydrogen, ORP and alkaline pH mean?

Hydrogen refers to dissolved molecular hydrogen, ORP relates to oxidation-reduction potential, and alkaline pH describes the water’s acidity or alkalinity level. WaterSource explains these during consultations and demonstrations.

Does WaterSource also filter the water?

Yes. WaterSource systems include filtration while also focusing on functional water properties beyond basic filtering.

How often do filters need to be changed?

Filter replacement depends on the selected model and usage level. The team can advise the recommended servicing schedule for your household or workplace.

Can WaterSource provide hot water?

Selected WaterSource models include hot-water convenience, including countertop options designed for daily family or office use.

Is acidic water meant for drinking?

No. Acidic water settings are generally positioned for external use, not drinking. The specialist will explain safe usage during the product demonstration.

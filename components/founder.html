Meet the founder

Hi, I’m Mrs Wee. At 83, my mission is clearer than ever.

To help people live longer, healthier and more vibrant lives.

From Orchard Road to a wellness mission.

In 1973, I started Evergreen Stationery, a cozy store near Orchard Road that blossomed into a trusted Singapore customeran brand — built with consistency, personal service and a deep belief that quality earns respect.

The health setback that changed my life.

After kidney stone removal surgery at age 60, I experienced fatigue, headaches, insomnia, skin sensitivity and memory concerns. That moment made me rethink my life’s purpose, both as an entrepreneur and as a person.

In 2009, I launched Evergreen Health.

Over the past 17 years, I have dedicated myself to healthier drinking water — hydrogen-rich, mineral-balanced water supported by negative ions, micro-molecule cluster positioning and wellness education.

What keeps me motivated?

  • To help others avoid the suffering I went through.
  • To make wellness technology safe, accessible and empowering.
  • To contribute to Singapore customer’s healthy-aging community.

What I bring.

  • A foundation in biological sciences.
  • Knowledge of wellness technology.
  • AI and Quantum wellness technology for non-invasive screening and personalised insight.

Looking forward to having you join me on this journey to better health.
